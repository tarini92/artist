Portfolio

Who?Me?

Writing code was my bread and butter, art and literature are my cheese and wine. I work my compulsive anxiety by swallowing life and regurgitating words, in form of poetry or comedy, based on circumstantial ease. I might scoff at the idea of God, but still believe he would have danced madly while creating this beautiful mess.I house many contradictions, because a person must only be put in a box, when dead.
